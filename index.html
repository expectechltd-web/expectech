Why Expectech

Local Expertise with Enterprise-Level Capability

Founded in Glasgow in 2023, Expectech Ltd was built on a simple belief: businesses deserve straightforward, expert IT support without the complexity or inflated costs of big consultancies.

We're a Scottish company that understands the local business landscape — from Glasgow's growing tech scene to SMEs across Scotland — while bringing the technical depth to tackle complex enterprise challenges.

  • Dedicated account managers — real people who know your business
  • Fast response times with UK-based support, never offshore call centres
  • Transparent, fixed-fee pricing with no hidden costs
  • Vendor-agnostic advice — we recommend what's right for you
  • Fully compliant with UK GDPR and ICO data protection requirements
  • Flexible contracts tailored to your business needs and budget
